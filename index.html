Sometimes I still can't believe I actually confessed to you—

it feels like a dream I never want to wake up from.

I remember falling for you, piece by piece:

the way your lips curve when you smile,

your chubby cheeks I always want to squish,

the soft sway of your hair when you flip it,

the way your hands move when you're talking,

your shoulders I want to rest my head on forever,

your delicate collar bones,

and that adorable little face that lights up my whole world.

Every moment with you feels like a cozy hug to my heart.

You're my home,

my safe place,

my everything—

and I love you more with every blink,

every breath,

every heartbeat.

Here's to us and all the silly, sweet, forever things.
